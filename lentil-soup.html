Healthy Middle Eastern Lentil Soup

Description

Healthy, Flavorful, and vegan! Packed full of veggies and fragrant spices!

Prep Time: 20 Minutes

Cook Time: 20 Minutes

Yield: 11 cups

Ingredients

  • 1 onion, diced (or sub 2 fat shallots)
  • 8 cloves of garlic, rough chopped
  • 3 table spoons of olive oil
  • 4 cups diced veggies, any mix of:
    • carrots
    • celery
    • fennel
    • parsnip
    • potato
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon salt, more to taste
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • 2 teaspoons coriander
  • 2 teaspoons curry powder
  • 1 turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon allspice (optional)
  • 1 bay leaf or 1 teaspoon of herbs de Provence or italian herbs
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 2 medium tomatoes, diced with their juices (or 14-ounce can of diced tomatoes with juices)
  • 4 flavorful cups of veggie stock
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 1/4 cup small black caviar lentils

Garnishes: drizzle of olive oil, lemon juice, chopped italian parsley or cilantro, diced tomato, diced tomatoes and optional chili flakes (aleppo chili is nice here) and serve with toasty pita bread

Instructions

  1. Saute onion and garlic in olice oil over medium-high heat until fragrant, about 3 minutes
  2. Add chopped veggies, lower heat and saute for 5 minutes
  3. Add the spices, salt and tomato paste, toasting them a bit
  4. Add the broth,and water and scrape up any brown bits
  5. Add the lentils, tomatoes bay leaves, bring to a simmer, cover, and simmer on medium-low or low heat until the lentils are cooked through. 20-30 minutes, depending on their size.
